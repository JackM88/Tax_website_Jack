We care for our truckers

We specialize in YOU, the driver. Whether you are an owner-operator or a company driver, you work hard for your money and we want you to keep as much of it as possible. The Trucking/Transportation Industry is a unique one and most drivers are unaware of all the deductions possible for them to take. We have developed our Driver Supplement to help our drivers identify all deductions possible when it comes to their taxes. We understand the trucking industry and will work with you to stay current on your taxes or help you get caught up from past years of not filing. Contact us today to see how we can help you save money!

Need to file your 2290? We can help! We can handle this for you or can facilitate you electronically filing your form. Get your stamped Schedule 1 quicker – contact us to learn more.

We want to help you succeed! If you are interested in TCNA helping you track your expenses throughout the year, please contact us regarding our bookkeeping and accounting services. We want to be THE tax firm for truck drivers which is why we welcome input from our clients always. Our supplement was developed with the help of our clients in the trucking industry and we are constantly looking for new ways we can help our clients more.
